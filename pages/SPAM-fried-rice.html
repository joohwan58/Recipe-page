SPAM fried rice!

Fried rice has a false perception of requiring lots of skill to get right. This is true if you want a top class meal to impress others, but if you just want something edible to get by as a broke college student, it takes no skill at all.

You will need:

  • leftover rice
  • SPAM
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • cokking oil
  • Soy sauce
